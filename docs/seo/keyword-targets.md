## Existing strike-zone / page-1 accidental wins (protect these)

"shiners", "topaz detailing" #20, "wrapstyle dubai" #6, "kenzie's ... detailing" #9). These are
navigational and low strategic value, but confirm listing pages CAN rank — the fix is building
category pages that target the service terms above, then internally linking the brand pages up to them.
