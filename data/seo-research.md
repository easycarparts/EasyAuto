## 0. TL;DR — what to build, in priority order

Ranked by **proven first-party demand (GSC) × winnability for a directory**:

1. **Car recovery / towing** — by far our strongest commercial signal: **~19,400 GSC impressions/12mo** across recovery+tow queries, already ranking page 1–2 on many (`fujairah recovery` pos 8.8, `recovery near me` pos 13, `sharjah recovery service` 5.2% CTR). 258 listings. **P3 flagship.** SERP is local-pack-heavy but we already rank — amplify.
2. **Car detailing / polishing** — **~4,200 impressions**, 296 listings, and the *only* money category where a horizontal directory (Bayut) already ranks organically ([bayut.com](https://www.bayut.com/mybayut/car-detailing-companies-in-dubai/)). Best organic-accessible field. **P3.**
3. **Car wash** — **~12,400 impressions** (`car wash near me` pos 2.36, 2,060 impr). High volume but head term owned by funded apps (CAFU, Justlife). Build the near-me page; expect long-tail wins. **P3.**
4. **Window tinting** — ~584 GSC impressions but high commercial intent + a strong informational hook (RTA tint law). **P3 + guide.**
5. **Car wrapping** — ~1,205 impressions (`car wrapping ajman` 581 impr pos 12). **P3.**
6. **PPF + ceramic** — only ~1,092 GSC impressions today (`ppf coating near me` 369 impr pos 62, `car detailing abu dhabi`-adjacent), BUT this is **the owner's money service** and a USD-1.2B UAE market ([kenresearch.com](https://www.kenresearch.com/uae-car-detailing-ppf-ceramic-coating-services-market)). Low current impressions = we have *no* ranking pages yet, not low demand. **P3 (near-me) + P4 (brand pages + guides) — strategic priority despite thin current GSC.**

**Two structural truths from the SERP research:** (a) the repeatable organic wins are *"best [service] in [city]"* editorial-style pages, not head money terms; (b) the local pack appears on every money query, so well-formed listings + internal linking matter as much as the landing copy.

**UAE regulations (note the SOURCE CONFLICTS — publish the conservative version):**
- **Window tint:** widely reported as up to **50% darkness** on side/rear, windshield clear, **AED 1,500 fine** ([cars24.ae](https://www.cars24.ae/news/en/buyers-guide/tinting-laws-in-the-uae/)); some newer shop content states VLT thresholds (front ≥70%, rear ≥30%) and AED 300–500 ([byduae.ae](https://www.byduae.ae/en/news/how-much-tint-is-allowed-in-dubai/)). **Publish the AED 1,500 / 50% version as primary, link RTA, note the alternative.** Medical exemption via RTA-approved doctor.
- **Wrapping / colour change:** reversible wrap may not need a Mulkiya change ([stationoneautos.com](https://stationoneautos.com/blog/car-wrapping-legal-dubai/)), but a **colour-change** wrap needs a CID/RTA colour-change permit + Mulkiya update within ~30 days, with an **AED 800** fine for unauthorised colour change ([uaeedge.com](https://uaeedge.com/rta-rules-for-car-wraps-in-dubai/)). Chrome/fluorescent/retroreflective wraps banned on private cars. **Publish the conservative version.**
- **Recovery fees:** no single official RTA schedule found — operator-reported ranges only; on highways only police-authorised recovery moves accident vehicles.
